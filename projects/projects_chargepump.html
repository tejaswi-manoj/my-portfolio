    Cadence Virtuoso

    Challenge

    Design a charge pump for a phase locked loop (PLL).

    Background

    I am currently leading the Phase Locked Loop (PLL) team at Silicon Jackets. My project was to design a charge pump for the PLL.

    Approach

    My goal was to build a low-voltage, low-power, simple solution ​ without an op-amp (since they increase area and consume power) and without output jumps​. In my final design, I chose a wide swing current mirror, with pump-up and pump-down circuits​. This provided a symmetric pump structure and reduced output jumps. I was able to successfully simulate the pumping up and pumping down of the output current in Cadence Virtuoso, after a few tweaks with the W/L ratios and design changes of the current mirror. I also coordinated the integration of the PLL block with other parts - the voltage-controlled oscillator, phase detector, and low pass filter.
