Hi! I am Tejaswi. I am a rising electrical engineering senior at Georgia Tech (GT).
I like to dabble in power electronics, VLSI systems, and AI/ML. I am also passionate about power systems engineering, particularly improving energy access for underserved communities.
Over the years, I have fallen in love with teaching (I have found no greater joy than the feeling of seeing a student's face light up when a concept finally clicks after I teach it to them), having served as a teaching assistant at GT for four courses across three semesters, including Circuit/Electronics lab (ECE 3043), Digital Design (ECE 2020), and Integral Calculus (MATH 1552).
I have previously interned at Tesla as a Failure Analysis Engineering intern in the Megafactory team and am currently interning at Apple as a Design Verification engineer. (These are wildly different fields - power electronics and chip design. But that's honestly what excites me about electrical engineering. There is simply too much to learn and too little time to explore everything.)
